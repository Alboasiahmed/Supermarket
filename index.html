About Us

Your Local Indian Grocery in Munno Para

Swagat India Groceries is a family-run store bringing the flavours of home to the northern suburbs of Adelaide. From everyday staples like basmati rice, atta and dals to fresh spices, snacks, sweets and frozen favourites, we stock the authentic Indian brands you know and love.

Pop in any day of the week — our friendly team is always happy to help you find exactly what you need, or to order in something special.
